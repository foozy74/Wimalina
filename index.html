The Platform

About Wimalina

Wimalina Limited is an independent corporate finance and strategic advisory platform serving selected clients, counterparties and opportunities across the United Kingdom, Continental Europe, the United States and beyond.

Founded in 2018, the firm draws on senior experience accumulated across banking, asset management, corporate development, investment-related environments and international business. This background informs an advisory model that is selective in mandate acceptance, measured in tone and practical in execution.

Established in the United Kingdom, Wimalina also serves as a suitable platform for substantial and, where required, sensitive assignments that benefit from a clear legal and operational framework.

Our Principles

Our Positioning

Wimalina operates at the intersection of corporate finance, strategic advisory and transaction support. We are particularly suited to mandates where commercial relevance, financial complexity and stakeholder sensitivity must be aligned with precision.

Our style is intentionally understated. We are not a broad-market intermediary, nor a volume-driven advisory business. We are most effective where the mandate is real, the counterpart universe is selective and the handling of process matters as much as the technical solution itself.

Our Method

Our Corporate Finance Approach

Corporate finance is a central pillar of Wimalina's advisory practice. We support selected clients in matters including capital raising, strategic funding concepts, M&A support, restructuring, balance-sheet related initiatives and wider transaction preparation.

We seek to ensure that a transaction is not only conceptually attractive, but also realistically executable. This includes development of the transaction logic, refinement of the strategic and financial narrative, coordination of advisers and counterparties, and disciplined support through critical stages.
